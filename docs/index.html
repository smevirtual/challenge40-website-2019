Our Principles and Goals

Virtual By Default. Serving Texas and beyond.

We embrace the Internet as the primary method of engagement with our members and the entire engineering community.

Building the next generation workforce

Manufacturing is moving fast - and evolving. For experienced professionals and students alike, it can be challenging to stay on top of it all.

Join our Meetup group to get notifications for when we launch multi-day workshops, snapshot webinars and other online educational events.

Open Source is our DNA

It may surprise you to learn that much of Smart Manufacturing is built on open source software and, recently, hardware.

We are entirely open source - from this website to the web applications that we use internally to the workshop materials we produce. Everything.

Engagement on your schedule

Your time is at a premium nowadays. Busy work schedules. Traffic. Family life. We understand.

All of our events are live-streamed, recorded and instantly available on YouTube. We also build unique, engaging and immersive web experiences to enrich and educate.

Conversations that are always on

Need some ideas for a class project? Want to ask the community for technical advice? Have any questions about a workshop?

No problem. Join our chat and subscribe to our subreddit. Start a conversation today. Open to the public.
